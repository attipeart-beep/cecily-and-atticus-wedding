Getting there

By car. There is ample parking on site, and you’re welcome to leave your car overnight.

By train. The nearest stations to the venue are Frome (15 minutes), Westbury (25 minutes) and Bath Spa (40 minutes). Both Westbury and Bath Spa have direct trains from London Paddington, with trains to Bath Spa running more frequently.

Good to know

Guests are welcome to use the facilities available to us at Babington. Some of these include:

Soho Health Club, indoor & outdoor pools, saunas, steam rooms and spa treatments.

Fitness & sport

A fully equipped gym, tennis and padel courts, and walking trails.

Dining & lounges

The Orangery restaurant, the House Kitchen & deli, the library, the snug and the bar.

Entertainment

A private cinema and a children’s club.
