Getting there

By car. There is ample parking on site, and you’re welcome to leave your car overnight.

By train. The nearest stations to the venue are Frome (15 minutes), Westbury (25 minutes) and Bath Spa (40 minutes). Both Westbury and Bath Spa have direct trains from London Paddington, with trains to Bath Spa running more frequently.

By air. Bristol Airport is roughly an hour away by car.

Taxis. We’d recommend pre-booking local taxis, especially for the journey home. We’ll share trusted numbers closer to the day.
