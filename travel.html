Babington House, Babington, Frome, Somerset, BA11 3RW

Where to lay your head

Accommodation

We have exclusive use of all rooms at the house and have arranged preferred rates for our guests. We’ll be in touch with further details should you wish to stay — simply let us know on your RSVP.

If the house is full or you’d prefer to stay nearby, there are lovely options in Frome (about 15 minutes away) and the surrounding villages. We’re happy to recommend a few.

Request a room on your RSVP

Getting there

How to find us

By car. Babington House is near Frome in Somerset. There is ample parking on site, and you’re welcome to leave your car overnight.

By train. The nearest stations are Frome (~15 mins) and Westbury (~25 mins), both with taxis available. Bath Spa (~40 mins) is well connected to London Paddington.

By air. Bristol Airport is roughly an hour away by car.

Taxis. We’d recommend pre-booking local taxis, especially for the journey home. We’ll share trusted numbers closer to the day.
