Getting there

By car. There is ample parking on site, and you’re welcome to leave your car overnight.

By train. The nearest stations to the venue are Frome (15 minutes), Westbury (25 minutes) and Bath Spa (40 minutes). Both Westbury and Bath Spa have direct trains from London Paddington, with trains to Bath Spa running more frequently.

Good to know

As mentioned, there is an optional breakfast on the Friday available to all guests, with checkout at 12:00pm. In and around this, guests are welcome to use the facilities available to us at Babington. Some of these include:

  • 🧖 Wellness & spa — Soho Health Club, indoor & outdoor pools, saunas, steam rooms and spa treatments
  • 🏋️ Fitness & sport — a fully equipped gym, tennis and padel courts, and walking trails
  • 🍽️ Dining & lounges — The Orangery restaurant, the House Kitchen & deli, and cosy bars
  • 🎬 Entertainment — a private cinema and a children’s club
