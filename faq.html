Ask us anything

When should I RSVP by?

Please let us know by 31st July 2026 so we can finalise numbers with the house. You can reply on the RSVP page.

What time should I arrive?

Check-in for guests staying over is from 3:00pm. Please be seated at St Margaret’s Church (on site) no later than 3:45pm, as the ceremony begins at 4:00pm.

What time does the evening end?

There’s no official curfew, so you’re welcome to carry on the evening with us for as long as you like.

What’s the dress code?

We kindly request smart winter attire. Most of the day is indoors, but there are short walks on site between the house, church and some accommodation where the ground underfoot varies, so we’d suggest wearing footwear/heels you’ll be comfortable in, as well as wearing layers for the season.

Where do I park?

There’s free parking on site. For guests not staying, you’re welcome to leave your car overnight and collect it the next day.

Can I bring a plus-one or my children?

Your invitation will let you know how many people it’s extended to. While our celebration is tailored for adults, your little ones are welcome if you would prefer to bring them along.

What if I have dietary requirements?

Please note any allergies or dietary needs on your RSVP and we’ll make sure the kitchen takes care of you.

Are you having a gift list?

Your presence is our present. We won’t be requesting any gifts from our guests.
