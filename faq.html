Q & A

When should I RSVP by?

Please let us know by 1st October 2026 so we can finalise numbers with the house. You can reply on the RSVP page.

What time should I arrive?

Check-in for guests staying over is from 3:00pm. Please be seated at St Margaret’s Church (on site) no later than 3:45pm — the ceremony begins at 4:00pm.

What’s the dress code?

Smart winter attire. It’s December in Somerset, so do bring a warm coat for the short walk between the church and the house.

Can I stay at the venue?

Yes — we have exclusive use of all rooms at the house and have arranged preferred rates for guests. Let us know on your RSVP and we’ll be in touch with details. See Travel & Stay for more.

Where do I park?

There’s free parking on site. You’re welcome to leave your car overnight and collect it the next day.

Can I bring a plus-one or my children?

Your invitation will specify who’s included. If you’re unsure, just ask us — we’re happy to clarify.

I have dietary requirements — what should I do?

Please note any allergies or dietary needs on your RSVP and we’ll make sure the kitchen takes care of you.

Are you having a gift list?

Your presence is the only present we need. If you’d like to give something, details will follow closer to the day — please don’t feel any obligation.

What’s the plan for the evening?

Drinks and canapés after the ceremony, a wedding breakfast with speeches, then dancing into the night. See the full running order.

Who do I contact with questions?

Drop us a line at cecandatti@gmail.com — we’re always happy to help.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you! x
